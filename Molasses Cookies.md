# Molasses Cookies

### Ingredients
- 3/4 cup butter or shortening
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup molasses
- 1 egg
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 2 cups flour
- 1/2 tsp cloves
- 1/2 tsp ginger
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp salt

### Directions
melt butter, remove and cool;
add sugar, molasses & egg - beat well;
add flour, soda, spices, salt;
mix well & chill;
form 1 inch balls - roll in granulated sugar;
bake @ 375 degrees 8-10 min
